Demo narrative

The product is designed around one practical business question: which campaign channel deserves budget, and why?

01 Brief

Compare assumptions

Budget, reach, engagement, conversion rate, brand fit and risk.

02 Decision

Score alternatives

Weighted scoring produces recommendation, confidence and ranked alternatives.

03 What-if

Stress test

Scenario analysis changes assumptions without overwriting the baseline decision.

04 Learn

Record actuals

Actual conversions and CPA close the loop with a learning insight.
